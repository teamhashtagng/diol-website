OIL AND GAS INDUSTRY

At DOODSS we deliver services at all stages of the oil and gas lifecycle. Our expertise covers all stages of the facility lifecycle, from concept design through to decommissioning. We have a proven track record of working with a full range of oil and gas sector clients. For years we’ve helped Oil and Gas companies improve their efficiency, while guiding them through technology improvements. We ensure projects are delivered safely, without incident or impact on the environment, reduce costs, on budget, boost efficiency and enhance productivity, while meeting corporate sustainability measures and compliance with governmental guidelines.

All of our clients receive a single point of contact service from an experience account manager. From tender response through to report sign off, we become an integral part of your project and guarantee that a fully joined up and smooth transition of project phases is realised. We also provide complete decision support, multidisciplinary design/projects, assurance and consultancy services to our clients, which in turn uniquely positioned us in the industry having deep expertise in oil and gas services.
