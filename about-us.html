ABOUT US

As a service company, Timproxy operates a diversified business with a special focus on Oil and Gas, Marine and the Power Industry. Our specialty cover equipment’s manufactured by major OEM’s. We are located in Lagos, Nigeria with an independent subsidiary in the United Kingdom. At Timproxy our main focus is to provide complete support to projects specializing in the selection, qualification and monitoring of our clients oil servicing needs. We have achieved a proven pedigree and track record of success, delivering significant and sustainable commercial value to a growing number of clients across a broad range of industry sectors. We also provide services from MRO to large-scale projects. We have a wealth of combined experience and knowledge in the actuation and automation of various types of valves and actuator combinations, enabling us to better serve our customers and ultimately save you time and money. We also provide metering and system services, Instrumentation and process control services, mechanical systems and monitoring security systems.
